  1. GUESTS, FOLLOWERS OR SUBSCRIBERS.

2.1 PUBLIC ACCOUNT. The Public Accounts feature enables you to view as guests, follow or subscribe to Public Accounts, which includes the Public Chat feature (each, a “Public Account”). When you visit a Public Account without Following it or Subscribing to it, you will be a “Guest” of a Public Account. As a Guest, if you click the “follow” button, you become a Follower of the Public Account and automatically a Subscriber of the Public Account as well. As a “Follower” of a Public Account, you may receive notices of updates to the content available in the Public Account and in the Public Chat included within the Public Account. As a “Subscriber” of a Public Account, you will be able to send and receive one-on-one messages with the Public Account. When you send a message to a Public Account you will automatically become a Subscriber of the Public Account with all that it entails. At any time, you can “unfollow” or “unsubscribe” or both “unfollow” and “unsubscribe” from any Public Account by clicking the “unfollow” and/or “unsubscribe” button.

2.2 PUBLIC CHAT. Public Chat, is a chat feature within the Public Account feature which enables Viber Users to share content, engage in conversations and create chats that are available to the general public between other Viber Users who may have a common interest or affiliation (each a “Public Chat”). the Public Chat feature provides the ability to digitally follow particular Public Chat and watch all content and other information introduced in it.

2.3 BOT. The Viber Bot feature enables you to subscribe to third parties’ Bot(s) and to send and receive one-on-one messages from the Bot(s) (the “Bot”). When you visit a Bot without subscribing to it, you will be a “Guest” of the Bot. As a Guest, if you click the “subscribe” button or any button presented to you by the Bot, or write a one-on-one message to the Bot, you will automatically become a “subscriber” of the Bot and be able to send and receive messages from the Bot. At any time, you can unsubscribe from any Bot by clicking the “unsubscribe” button.

2.4 COMMUNITY. Communities are group chats through which Viber Users can exchange messages and engage with one another and can include up to 1 billion Viber Users (a “Community”). If you join you will become a “Member” of such Community. As a part of a Community, Viber Users can share content, engage in conversations, and share the Community with other Viber Users. Viber Users can leave a Community at any time by selecting the “Leave and delete” button. All Members of the Community can see the username and the photo of all the other Members of the Community.

Please be aware that the content of the conversations exchanged between Viber Users and Administrators through the Community, Public Account feature or the Bot feature, are not end-to-end encrypted and may be stored by Viber as well as the relevant Administrator(s) and their third party providers.
